== Description ==
Releated Posts with thumbnails of Aitu is developed and maintained by 51aitu.com to display related posts with thumbnails  within and between many sites. 
"爱徒相关文章插件"是爱徒网络的智能推荐产品, 它可以在短时间内提升网站的访问量. 能自动抓取你博客的内容, 以缩略图的形式展示相关度最高的文章. 其关联选取算法依据用户行为, 文章内容, 文章标签, 时间等，可优化高质量文章的点击率. 此插件主要面向中文网站. 详情请查看: http://tuijian.51aitu.com/


<h4>主要功能</h4>
   -迅速提高网站流量
   -增加内链外链
   -优质内容迅速扩展到各网站展示
   -智能分析站内相关性
   -显示相关文章
   -操作简单 

Releated Posts with thumbnails of Aitu can quickly increase your blog's page views within short time. It indexed your site and automatically finds out related posts with thumbnails. Blogs using "Aitu Related Posts" will automatically integrate with 51aitu.com, and have their articles spread out to more readers. And this is mainly for Chinese blogs. For more information please visit: http://tuijian.51aitu.com

<h4>Features</h4>
*analyze the relation of posts within the site
*display the related post with thumbnails within the site
*exchange traffic between different sites  

== Installation ==

1. 下载Aitu-related-posts.zip.
1. 从插件管理界面上传插件.
1. 点击`现在安装`.
1. 在后台管理面板里的`设置` -> `爱徒`可以找到插件的设置选项.

或

1. 解压缩下载的Aitu-related-posts.zip.
1. 将解压缩后得到的文件夹上传到WordPress `/wp-content/plugins/` 目录下.
1. 在WordPress的后台`管理插件`页面启用`爱徒相关文章插件`.


1. Download aitu-related-posts.zip.
1. Upload the zipped file directly in wordpress admin page.
1. Click `Install Now`.

OR

1. Download aitu-related-posts.zip and unzip it.
1. Copy the unzipped folder `Aitu-related-posts` to `/wp-content/plugins/`
1. Activate the plugin.
1. Go to `Settings -> 爱徒` and customize the options you want.
